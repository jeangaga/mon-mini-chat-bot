Why it matters: The hold at 4.00% was expected, but the conditions justifying it — housing re-accelerating, labour tight, inflation above target — define the easing threshold for the full monitoring window. Understanding what Norges Bank is waiting for (housing cooling, inflation sustainably toward target, labour easing) is essential for reading the subsequent CPI and activity releases.
Norway Manufacturing Output November (released 8 Jan 2026)
Why it matters: The +2.4% m/m surge against -0.9% consensus removes the industrial-weakness argument for early easing and establishes Norway as an outperformer versus the broader DM manufacturing softness visible in the same period.
